Overview

LogBook ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal work journal application that helps users log, reflect on, and gain insights from their work. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We take privacy seriously. Your journal entries are your personal reflections — we do not read them, sell them, or use them for advertising.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

Account information

  • Email address and password (used for authentication via Supabase)
  • Display name, logbook name, and organisation (entered during onboarding)

Journal entries

  • Date, hours worked, energy level, and location
  • Free-text reflections: what you worked on, learned, blockers, ideas, and tomorrow's plan

Usage data

  • Basic analytics collected by Vercel (page views, error logs) — no personally identifiable information

Payment information

  • Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
  • We store only your Stripe customer ID to manage your subscription status.

Google Drive Integration

How we use Google Drive

LogBook's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

When you choose to connect Google Drive, LogBook requests access to create and manage files that LogBook itself creates (OAuth scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file). This scope does not grant access to any other files in your Drive.

Specifically, we use this access to:

  • Create a folder named "LogBook" in your Google Drive
  • Upload PDF exports of your journal entries to that folder

We do not:

  • Read any files in your Drive other than those LogBook created
  • Delete or modify existing files in your Drive
  • Share your Drive files with any third party
  • Use your Drive data to train AI models
  • Transfer your Drive data to third parties for advertising purposes

Your Google OAuth refresh token is stored securely in our database and is used solely to perform uploads on your behalf. You can disconnect Google Drive at any time from the History page — this immediately deletes your refresh token from our systems. Files already uploaded to your Drive are not affected.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the LogBook service
  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To generate PDF exports of your journal entries
  • To power the AI Chat feature (your entries are sent to Anthropic's Claude API as context — see below)
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. password reset) via Supabase Auth

AI Chat (Pro feature)

When you use AI Chat, your journal entries are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. Anthropic processes this data according to their Privacy Policy. Your data is not used to train Anthropic's models under our API agreement.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a cloud database provider, with row-level security enforced — meaning your data is only accessible by your account. Supabase stores data on AWS infrastructure in the EU (Europe West) region.

Your application is served via Vercel's global CDN. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. All data at rest is encrypted by Supabase.

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong password and keep your credentials safe.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following sub-processors, strictly to provide the service:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI Chat responses (Pro users only, on demand)
  • Google — Drive integration (only when you explicitly connect it)

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from within the app — this permanently and immediately removes all your journal entries, profile information, and OAuth tokens from our systems. Your Stripe subscription (if active) will also be cancelled.

If you need assistance with account deletion, contact us at haroldponte2003@gmail.com.
